Blackjack Rules


  • Aces may be counted as 1 or 11 points, 2 to 9 according to pip value, and tens and face cards count as 10 points.

  • Player and Dealer starts with 2 cards each.

  • If the player’s cards add up to more than 21, then the player lose and must start again. The game is over.

  • If the player’s cards add up to exactly 21, the player has a blackjack, and the game is over. Player win.

  • If a player’s cards add up to 21 or less, but has already been dealt five cards, then the game is over.

  • Otherwise, the player can choose to get another card or stop playing.
